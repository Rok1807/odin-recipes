Jollof Spaghetti Recipe

Jollof Spaghetti

Jollof Spaghetti is an alternative to Jollof rice.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • pepper mix
  • tomato paste
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Place your pot on heat and add vegetable oil
  2. Blend the onions, add it together with the pepper mix and tomato paste (leave for 3 minutes)
  3. Add chilli flakes,curry, salt and thyme
  4. Add water to the fried sauce and bring it to boil
  5. Add spaghetti and leave for ten minutes
  6. Your delicious meal is ready to be served.
